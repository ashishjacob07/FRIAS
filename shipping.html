Delivery Information

Once you have decided what to buy, you can choose one of our delivery methods

-Premium delivery

Premium is our quickest and most flexible delivery option. All of the products will be delivered by a courier to the place and time of your choice. Premium delivery takes at least 4 hours(if you are local) and 2 days for another cities, starting from purchase confirmation.

-Regualr delivery

Delivery time is 1 day(if you are local) and up to 7 days for another cities. Our operators will contact you to ask for address and tell you how much time the delivery will take approximately.
